What is this page?

Matrix Arbitrage performs algorithmic crypto arbitrage trading. The arbitral strategy allows Neosify to provide liquidity by taking advantage of price differences of different crypto pairs across decentralized exchanges and generate profit.

On the Matrix Technology Arbitrage page, you see a price chart and ongoing trades of wrapped Bitcoin and wrapped Ethereum across five networks – Ethereum (WBTC and WETH), Avalanche (WBTC.E and WETH), Polygon (WBTC and WETH), BSC (BTCB and WETH), and Fantom (WBTC and WETH).

On the left, you see a real-time BTCUSDT or ETHUSDT price chart. On the right, you can track the price of wrapped Bitcoin and wrapped Ethereum across the five networks – Ethereum, Avalanche, Polygon, BSC and Fantom. At the page bottom, you track relevant sell and buy orders.

Yes, you can choose a cryptocurrency like WETH and track successful trades carried out across the networks.

We send cryptocurrency from one blockchain to another via cross-chain Bridges - the protocol that solves the blockchain incompatibility problem. Apart from that, Bridge fees are particularly low in comparison with the usual blockchain transfer fees.
